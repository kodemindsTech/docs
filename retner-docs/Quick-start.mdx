# Welcome to Retner

Retner is an all-in-one customer engagement platform that helps businesses automate and personalize communication across WhatsApp, Email, SMS, and more.

## What You Can Do with Retner

**Templates** — Create WhatsApp, Email, SMS, and RCS message templates

**Audience Management** — Build static lists and dynamic segments of your contacts

**Campaigns** — Send targeted broadcasts via WhatsApp, Email, and SMS

**Journey Builder** — Automate multi-step customer journeys with triggers and actions

**AI Assistant** — Deploy AI-powered chatbots for WhatsApp Business

**Analytics** — Track revenue, open rates, click rates, and conversions

## Step 1: Access the Dashboard

After logging in, you will land on the **Dashboard**. The dashboard gives you a snapshot of your last 30 days activity including total revenue, orders, abandoned cart recoveries, and messages sent.

You can quickly navigate to key actions from the dashboard quick cards: New Campaign, New Automation, New Template, Chat Panel, and New Segment.

## Step 2: Create a Message Template

Before sending any campaign or automation, you need a message template.

Go to **Templates** in the left sidebar and choose your channel:
- **WhatsApp Template** — Pre-approved message formats for WhatsApp Business
- **Email Template** — Branded email designs using drag-and-drop or HTML editor
- **SMS Template** — Simple text message templates
- **RCS Template** — Rich Communication Services templates

## Step 3: Build Your Audience

Go to **Audience** in the sidebar to manage who receives your messages:
- **Contacts** — View and manage all your contacts (1,000+ supported)
- **List Segment** — Create a fixed list by uploading a CSV file
- **Live Segment** — Create a dynamic segment with filter conditions that auto-updates

## Step 4: Launch a Campaign

Go to **Campaigns** in the sidebar. Choose WhatsApp, Email, or SMS. Enter a campaign name, select your target audience (a segment or static list), choose a message template, and click **Submit** to send or schedule.

## Step 5: Automate with Journey Builder

Go to **Journey Builder** in the sidebar. Click **+ Create Flow** and choose a pre-defined journey template or **Build From Scratch**. Add action blocks (WhatsApp, SMS, Email, Time Delay), configure triggers, and click **Save Automation** to activate.
